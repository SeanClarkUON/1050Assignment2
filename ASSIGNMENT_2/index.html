What this site is about

This website is designed to to show all the
restaurants within the Newcastle district that
have Online food ordering and Delivery.


Below you will find a description of each cuisine type, as well
as the main types of food they cook with. However, this is only
a few of the ingredients used and may vary between each restaurant.

Cuisine Descriptions:

Asian:









Asian cusine includes several major regional cuisines:
East Asian, Southeast Asian, South Asian,
Central Asian and Middle Eastern.
Ingredients common to many cultures in the
East and Southeast regions of the continent include:

Rice

Ginger

Sesame Seeds

Chilies

Dried Onions

Soy

Tofu
















Burgers:

A Hamburger or Burger is a sandwich consisting of
one or more cooked patties of ground meet,
usally beef, placed inside a sliced bread roll or bun.
Ingredients include:
Meat Patty (Mushroom or Tofu for vegetarians)

Cheese

Bacon

Onions

Pickles

Various Sauces

Many restaurants allow you to cusomise what you would like on a Burger













Italian:

Italian Cuisine is food typical from Italy.
It has developed through centuries of social
and economic changes, with roots stretching to antiquity.
Italian Cuisine has a great variet of different ingredients.
Among the most common are:

Pasta

Various Vegetables

Cheeses

Various Fish

Olives

Various Meats
Plus a wide of other ingredients








BBQ andGrill:

Barbecue is done slowly over low, indirect heat and the food is
flovored by the smoking process, while gilling is generally done
quikcly over moderate-tohigh direct heat that produces little smoke.
Mainly used for cooking various meats,
but also can be done to vegetables as well.
Types include:

Sausages

Steak

Various Vegetables

Almost any type of meats






Indian

Indian Cuisine encompasses a wide variety of reginal and
traditional cuisines native to India.Given the rage of diversity
in soil type,culture, ethnic groups and occupations,
these cuisines vary substantially from each other.
Some of the most common ingredients used are:

Spices

Herbs

Vegetables

Fruits








Thai:

Thai Cuisine is the nation cuisine of Thailand.
Balance, detail and variety are of paramount significance to Thai chefs
Thai cooking places emphasis on lightly prepared
dishes with strong aromatic compnents and a spicy edge.
Ingredients include:

Rice

Noodles

Pastas

Sauces

Vegetables

Fruits

Among other items.
